Online Threat Detection System

The proliferation of social media and online communication channels has facilitated the dissemination of false information, spam, and phishing attempts on an unprecedented scale. To counter these malicious activities, we have designed and implemented a sophisticated machine learning-powered system capable of identifying and thwarting fake news, email spam, insurance fraud, and phishing URLs and we are also providing a chatbot that will be able to solve the queries of end-users
